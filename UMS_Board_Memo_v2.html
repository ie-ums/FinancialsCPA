3Findings & Risk Snapshot
Strength
Strong profit turning point.Gross margin moved up from 4.0% to 9.6% in one quarter. Q1 2026 net profit (PLN 559K / EUR 132K) is already higher than the full 2025 result.
Positive
High Risk
Heavy reliance on one supplier.ZIK provides 90% of UMS costs. Risk drops once UMS buys ZIK (planned EUR 6M at 4.0x EBITDA — bottom of EU range).
Mitigated
Medium
Very tight working cash.Net working capital is only PLN 2K. 88% of revenue is foreign in EUR/USD with no FX protection. FX cover is the cheapest big-risk fix.
Action Now
4Recommendations
  1. 30 daysHire an official auditor for FY 2025 and prepare a cash flow report under Polish accounting rules (PSR).
  2. 60 daysOpen a revolving credit line of PLN 2–3 million (about EUR 0.5–0.7 million) at PKO BP or mBank.
  3. 90 daysOpen EUR/PLN forward contracts to cover at least 50% of next-year foreign revenue. Close the ZIK acquisition.
5Conclusion

Position: Satisfactory — with conditions. UMS is ready for trade financing and a working-capital line of credit right now. A long-term loan above EUR 1.2 million needs three things first: an official audit, a cash flow report, and an FX protection plan.
